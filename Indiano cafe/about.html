About our cafe

Our cafe is a cozy, welcoming spot that's the perfect blend of modern comfort and rustic charm, with an emphasis on high-quality coffee and a relaxing atmosphere. Our Story

Indiano Cafe started with a simple dream: to bring the authentic taste of traditional Indian beverages and snacks to the modern cafe scene. Our founders traveled across the country to learn the age-old secrets of brewing the perfect masala chai and roasting the finest coffee beans. Today, we stand as a bridge between heritage and contemporary cafe culture, striving for excellence daily.

Ethically Sourced

We believe great coffee starts long before it reaches your cup. That's why we partner directly with local farmers who practice sustainable agriculture. Every bean, tea leaf, and spice used in Indiano Cafe is ethically sourced, ensuring fair trade and superior quality that you can taste in every sip.
